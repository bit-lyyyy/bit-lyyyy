Motorcycle Engine Modifications

I bored out my motorcycle engine to increase the displacement from 125cc to 186cc. I installed a new piston, camshaft, piston, crank shaft, cylinder head, cam chain, valves, fuel injectors, oil cooler, and programmed a new engine control unit (ECU).
