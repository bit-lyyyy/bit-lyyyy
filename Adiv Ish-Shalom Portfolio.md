### 30kW Electric Bicycle - The Fastest in NYC
I modified a Recon electric bicycle, adding a new controller and wiring harness. The motor tune I created is running 30kW of power making this one of the fastest electric bicycles in NYC. It has been raced against many bikes and it hasn't lost once. The stock torque plates which interface the motor axle to the swing arm weren't good enough which ended up twisting the axle. # **Motorcycle Engine Modifications**
I bored out my motorcycle engine to increase the displacement from 125cc to 186cc. I installed a new piston, camshaft, piston, crank shaft, cylinder head, cam chain, valves, fuel injectors, oil cooler, and programmed a new engine control unit (ECU). The Bike ran more efficently, increased power from 7hp to 21hp, increased top speed from 60mph to 85mph, and I programmed a mode to pop/shoot flames out of my exhaust on command.
